# RemoveGroupIndicator
Removes the *HUD_GroupIndicator* from the *GroupIndicators* array and from the
map.  

Target is *HUD_LargeMap* or *HUD_LargeMap_Random*.  

## Node

## Inputs
|Name       |Type       |Description            |
|-----------|-----------|-----------------------|
|**GroupID**|*Integer*  |The ID of the group.   |

## Outputs
**None**
