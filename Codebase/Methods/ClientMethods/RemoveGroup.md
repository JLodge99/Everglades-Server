# RemoveGroup
Removes the given group from the *ActiveGroups* array.  

Target is *EvergladesGameMode*.  

## Node

## Inputs
|Name       |Type           |Description            |
|-----------|---------------|-----------------------|
|**In**     |*Exec*         |Execution pin.         |
|**Group**  |*SwarmGroup*   |The group to remove.   |

## Outputs
**None**
