|Variable           |Type       |Description                    |
|-------------------|-----------|-------------------------------|
|**GroupID**        |*Integer*  |The ID of the group.           |
|**IconBase_Blue**  |*Image*    |Image for a blue team group.   |
|**IconBase_Red**   |*Image*    |Image for a red team group.    |
|**Text_ID**        |*TextBlock*|The displayed group ID.        |
