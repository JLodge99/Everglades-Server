# ToggleSimplifiedMap
Toggles the texture of the large map and minimap based on whether *Simplified*
is **True** or **False**.  

Target is *SpectatorHUD* or *SpectatorHUD_Random*.  

## Node

## Inputs
|Name           |Type       |Description                        |
|---------------|-----------|-----------------------------------|
|**Simplified** |*Boolean* |Whether to use the simplified map. |

## Outputs
**None**
