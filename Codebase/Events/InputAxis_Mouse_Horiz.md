# InputAxis_Mouse_Horiz
Adjusts yaw of spectator to the left or right depending on *AxisValue*.

Target is *EvergladesSpectatorPawn*.  

## Node

## Inputs
**None**

## Outputs
|Name           |Type   |Description                                |
|---------------|-------|-------------------------------------------|
|**AxisValue**  |*Float*|Current value of the *Mouse_Horiz* axis.   |
