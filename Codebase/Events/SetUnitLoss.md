# SetUnitLoss
Sets the text and plays the animation associated with a player losing units during a 
bout of combat.  

Target is *HUD_UnitLoss*.  

## Node

## Inputs
|Name           |Type       |Description                        |
|---------------|-----------|-----------------------------------|
|**UnitsLost**  |*Integer*  |The amount of units a player lost. |

## Outputs
**None**
