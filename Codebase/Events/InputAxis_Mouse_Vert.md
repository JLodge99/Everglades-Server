# InputAxis_Mouse_Vert
Adjusts pitch of spectator up or down depending on *AxisValue*.  

Target is *EvergladesSpectatorPawn*.  

## Node

## Inputs
**None**

## Outputs
|Name           |Type   |Description                                |
|---------------|-------|-------------------------------------------|
|**AxisValue**  |*Float*|Current value of the *Mouse_Vert* axis.    |
