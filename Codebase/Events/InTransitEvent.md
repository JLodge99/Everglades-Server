# InTransitEvent
Event called every turn to process movements of groups that are in transit.  

Target is *EvergladesGameMode*.  

## Node

## Inputs
|Name           |Type       |Description            |
|---------------|-----------|-----------------------|
|**SwarmID**    |*Integer*  |The group's ID.        |
|**TargetNode** |*Integer*  |The target node's ID.  |

## Outputs
**None**
