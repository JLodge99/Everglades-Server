# InputAxis_Elevate
Moves spectator pawn up or down in world space depending on *AxisValue*.  

Target is *EvergladesSpectatorPawn*.  

## Node

## Inputs
**None**

## Outputs
|Name           |Type   |Description                            |
|---------------|-------|---------------------------------------|
|**AxisValue**  |*Float*|Current value of the *Elevate* axis.   |
